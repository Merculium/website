Merculium R&D

Innovation at the intersection of science, nature, and art.

About Us

Merculium R&D is a multidisciplinary studio focused on innovation in cosmetics, ecology, and sound-based research. We combine scientific precision with creative exploration to design sustainable systems, products, and experiences.

Cosmetic R&D

  • Formulation of natural and functional cosmetics
  • Product development and scaling for market launch
  • Production technology and documentation
  • Research on raw materials (non-commercial phase)

Consulting & Creative Strategy

  • Branding, marketing audits, SEO and e-commerce
  • Graphic design and social media strategy

Ecological & Permaculture Research

  • Experiments with plant growth and sound stimulation
  • Development of low-impact agriculture

Sound & Regeneration

  • Research on sound frequencies and plant/human response
  • Design of interdisciplinary workshops and events
  • Exploration of vibrational therapy and eco-art
